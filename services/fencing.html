Straight lines that last.

New builds go in plumb and on proper footings so they don't lean a season later. Repairs mean swapping the rotten posts and boards, re-hanging the gates that drag, and getting the line true again.

Want it to last and look its best? We'll clean, sand and re-stain or paint so the whole run matches and the wood is protected.

A good fence is half the look of a property and all of its privacy. We build new runs, fix the sagging and broken sections, and refresh tired wood so the whole line stands straight and looks sharp.

  • Post, board and rail repairs

  • Site left clear — old material hauled away
