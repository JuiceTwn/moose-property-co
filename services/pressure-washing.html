Pressure Washing

Drives, walks and siding brought back to clean. Years of grime, gone.

Get a free quote (519) 706-3325Call or text

The right pressure for the surface.

Concrete, pavers, wood and siding all want a different touch. We match the pressure and method to the surface so it comes up clean without etching the concrete or splintering the deck.

It's the fastest way to make a property look years younger, and it sets up a fresh seal or stain if that's next on the list.

Grime builds up so slowly you stop noticing it, until it's gone. We pressure-wash drives, walkways, patios and siding back to how they looked new, lifting the green, the black streaks and the ground-in dirt.

What's included
  • Driveways, walks and patios washed clean
  • Siding and soffits brought back to colour
  • Decks and fences refreshed at safe pressure
  • Green growth, black streaks and grime lifted
  • Surface-matched pressure, no damage
  • Surfaces left ready to seal or stain
