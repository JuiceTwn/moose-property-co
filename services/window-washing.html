Window Washing

Bright, streak-free glass that makes the whole place look newer.

Get a free quote (519) 706-3325Call or text

The detail most people skip.

It isn't just the glass. We wipe the frames, tracks and sills so the whole window looks clean, not just the middle pane. Screens get a clean too where they need it.

Ground-floor or upper storeys, we have the reach and the gear to do it safely, and we treat the inside of your home with the same care as the outside.

Clean glass changes how a house feels. More light in, a sharper look out. We wash windows inside and out, clear the frames and sills, and leave the glass streak-free, not smeared.

What's included
  • Interior and exterior glass washed streak-free
  • Frames, tracks and sills wiped down
  • Screens cleaned where needed
  • Upper-storey windows reached safely
  • Cobwebs cleared from corners and eaves
  • Floors and sills left dry and tidy inside
