Lawns & Edging

A clean, manicured lawn — without ever touching the mower.

Get a free quote (519) 706-3325Call or text

Cut to a standard, on a schedule.

Mowing height matters more than people think. We cut to suit your grass and the season so it stays thick, green, and healthy instead of scalped and stressed. Clean edges and crisp lines do the rest — that manicured look that makes a yard read as cared-for.

Set it and forget it. We come on a schedule that suits your lawn, rain or shine, and you never have to think about it again.

A sharp lawn sets the tone for the whole property. We keep yours cut to the right height, edged clean along every walk and bed, and trimmed everywhere the mower can't reach. Every clipping leaves with us, so the place looks finished the moment we pull away.

What's included
  • Mowing at the right height for your grass and season
  • String-trimming around trees, beds, posts and fences
  • Crisp edging along driveways, walks and garden beds
  • Every clipping cleared and taken with us
  • Blown-down walks and drive before we leave
  • Weekly or bi-weekly schedules to suit the yard
