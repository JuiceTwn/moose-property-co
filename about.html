How Moose started.

We are Justin Colwell and Chase Vienneau, two guys from right here in Grey-Bruce. Moose started the way a lot of good things do: we were tired of watching properties get done halfway, by crews that rushed the job, cut corners and left the customer chasing them. We figured we could do it better, so we did.

We are young and we are hungry, and we are building this from the ground up. That works in your favour. When it is our name on the truck and our reputation in these towns, there is no incentive to cut a corner. Every job we take is one we want to be proud of, because around here word travels and the work is the whole business.

No call centre, no franchise, no being passed from person to person. You get the two of us, the same standard every visit, and a straight answer when you pick up the phone.

  • The owners on the job, not a rotating crew
  • One point of contact, start to finish
  • The same standard every single visit
